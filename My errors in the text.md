### 1. Мини-письмо каждый день (10–15 минут)

Не “сочинения”, а короткие тексты на 5–8 предложений. Главное — регулярность.

Примеры заданий:

- “What I did yesterday” (did, past simple)
- “My usual day” (do/does, present simple)
- “Things I enjoy doing” (gerund)
- “What I want to do” (infinitive)
- “Using ‘get’ in different meanings”
- “Describe a place” (артикли + to be)

👉 Важно: **каждый день фокус на одной теме**, а не всё сразу.

Yesterday I did my work. I stayed at home. I woke up at 11–12 AM. My time zone is Moscow. Then I did my morning routine and watched videos on YouTube. After that, I studied English, specifically “gerund and infinitive,” and wrote an abstract about it. Next, I posted my abstract in my Telegram channel and talked with my audience. Then I recorded materials for my course and edited them. After that, I watched YouTube videos again and had dinner. A bit later, I had a video meeting with my student on Discord.
